Radio 1851

Radio 1851 is Saint Joseph’s University’s student-run radio station. Radio 1851 plays a variety of genres, including indie rock, rap/hip-hop, country, and electric dance music. The station began in 1922 as WSJR, the first college radio station to broadcast on AM, and then moved to FM in the 80s. After a brief hiatus, Radio 1851 returned in 2010 to broadcast exclusively online.

Our studio is located on the 3rd floor of Simpson Hall on Saint Joseph University’s Campus, Philadelphia, PA, 19131.
